    Subaru Equity Shield

      • Subaru Equity Shield GAP

        When the unexpected happens, and your car is totaled, or stolen and not recovered, the last thing you want to worry about is paying an outstanding balance on your loan for a vehicle you no longer have. Subaru Equity Shield GAP covers all or most of the difference between your auto insurance settlement and what you may still owe on your loan.

        Optimum Vehicle Protection (OVP)

        • Protect yourself from the unexpected! Tire & Wheel Protection

          covers costs related to the repair or replacement of tires, and/or the replacement of wheels structurally damaged
           by road hazards, such as potholes, nails and debris. There are unlimited claims and no mileage limitations for Tire & Wheel Protection during your agreement and no associated deductibles.

          And when selected, Optional Cosmetic Coverage repairs nicks and scrapes on wheels

          that may occur when brushing against a curb. Cosmetic Coverage repairs are limited to a total of eight (8) individual wheel repairs during the term of the agreement.

        • In addition to being unsightly, dents and dings can harm the resale or trade-in value of your vehicle. Maintain your vehicle’s value and appearance with Subaru Dent Protection

          . Dent Protection can permanently remove door dings and minor dents without harming your vehicle’s factory finish, keeping your Subaru picture perfect for years to come. Repairs can be performed at your Subaru retailer, your home, or your office. There are unlimited claims and no mileage limitations during your agreement and no associated deductibles.

        • Maintain a clear view of the road ahead. Minor chips and cracks can obstruct your view and detract from your vehicle’s appearance. Subaru Windshield Protection

           repairs chips and cracks in your front windshield caused by propelled rocks or other road hazards during normal driving conditions. Windshield Protection helps keep you worry-free and your outlook positive. Repairs can be performed at your Subaru retailer, your home, or your office. There are no limits on occurrences or claim benefits, no mileage limitations and no associated deductibles during your agreement. Windshield Protection is not available for purchase in Florida.

          When selected, Optional Windshield Replacement

          adds two (2) replacements of the front windshield during the agreement term. Windshield Replacement coverage is only available for Subaru models and is not available in all states.

          . Whether your keys are lost, damaged, or locked inside your car, we’ve got you covered. Coverage includes the cost of a replacement key and/or transponder, 24-hour vehicle lock-out assistance and home lockout assistance if your household keys are lost with your covered vehicle key.

          There are no mileage limitations or associated deductibles.

        Subaru Mobility Assist

        No matter the challenge, Subaru designs vehicles that give drivers a strong sense of control, maneuverability and security. That’s why we created Subaru Mobility Assist, a program that helps Subaru drivers with disabilities get the vehicles modifications they need in order to keep them safe and active. Through Subaru Mobility Assist, drivers get up to $1,000 reimbursement for necessary vehicle modifications on eligible Subaru vehicles.
